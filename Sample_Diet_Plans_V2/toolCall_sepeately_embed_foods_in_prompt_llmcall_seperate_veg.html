7-Day Vegetarian Meal Plan

Food List & Restrictions

Allowed Foods: Almonds, Artichoke, Arugula, Blueberry, Broccoli, Butter, Cabbage, Cauliflower, Chard, Eggplant, Eggs, Garlic, Ginger, Hazelnuts, Kale, Lettuce, Macadamia nuts, Mushrooms, Onion, Pecans, Pine nuts, Sesame seeds, Spinach, Strawberry, Tomatoes, Walnuts

Allergies: null

Other Food Restrictions: Vegetarian

Day 1

Daily Summary

Total Calories: 1657 kcal

Nutrition: Carbs: 51g | Protein: 52g | Fat: 146g | Fiber: 24g

Pre-Meal Appetizer: Arugula and Walnut Salad (12:30 PM)

Main Ingredient: Arugula Portion: 1 small bowl (approx. 75g) Nutrition: 248 Calories, 5g Carbs, 5g Protein, 25g Fat, 3g Fiber
Recipe: Toss 50g of fresh arugula with 20g of crushed walnuts. Drizzle with 1 tablespoon of melted butter as dressing.

Breakfast: Mushroom Masala Omelette (8:00 AM)

Main Ingredient: Mushrooms Portion: 1 omelette Nutrition: 447 Calories, 7g Carbs, 23g Protein, 38g Fat, 2g Fiber
Recipe: Whisk 3 large eggs. In a pan, melt 2 tablespoons of butter. Sauté 1 minced clove of garlic, 1/4 chopped onion, and 100g of sliced mushrooms until soft. Pour in the eggs and cook until set. Serve hot.

Lunch: Cauliflower 'Rice' Pilaf (1:00 PM)

Main Ingredient: Cauliflower Portion: 1 medium bowl (approx. 300g) Nutrition: 461 Calories, 15g Carbs, 10g Protein, 43g Fat, 8g Fiber
Recipe: Grate 200g of cauliflower to a rice-like consistency. In a pan, melt 2 tablespoons of butter. Sauté 1/2 chopped onion, 1 minced clove of garlic, and 1/2 inch of grated ginger. Add the cauliflower 'rice' and cook for 5-7 minutes. Stir in 30g of crushed almonds.

Dinner: Garlic Creamed Spinach (7:30 PM)

Main Ingredient: Spinach Portion: 1 bowl (approx. 250g) Nutrition: 375 Calories, 12g Carbs, 8g Protein, 36g Fat, 7g Fiber
Recipe: In a pot, wilt 250g of fresh spinach. Drain excess water and chop finely. In a pan, melt 3 tablespoons of butter. Sauté 2 minced cloves of garlic and 1/4 chopped onion. Add the chopped spinach and cook for 5 minutes until creamy.

Snack: Strawberries with Macadamia Nuts (4:00 PM)

Main Ingredient: Strawberry Portion: 1 small bowl (130g) Nutrition: 256 Calories, 12g Carbs, 3g Protein, 24g Fat, 4g Fiber
Recipe: Combine 100g of sliced strawberries with 30g of whole or halved macadamia nuts.
Day 2

Daily Summary

Total Calories: 1689 kcal

Nutrition: Carbs: 54g | Protein: 49g | Fat: 149g | Fiber: 25g

Pre-Meal Appetizer: Simple Lettuce and Tomato Salad (12:30 PM)

Main Ingredient: Lettuce Portion: 1 bowl (approx. 200g) Nutrition: 156 Calories, 9g Carbs, 2g Protein, 13g Fat, 3g Fiber
Recipe: Chop 100g of lettuce and 1 medium tomato. Toss together with 1 tablespoon of melted butter.

Breakfast: Indian Scrambled Eggs (Egg Bhurji) (8:00 AM)

Main Ingredient: Eggs Portion: 1 serving Nutrition: 465 Calories, 9g Carbs, 21g Protein, 39g Fat, 2g Fiber
Recipe: In a pan with 2 tablespoons of butter, sauté 1/2 chopped onion, 1 chopped small tomato, 1 minced clove of garlic, and 1/2 inch of grated ginger. Whisk 3 large eggs and add to the pan. Scramble until cooked through.

Lunch: Smoked Eggplant Mash (Baingan Bharta style) (1:00 PM)

Main Ingredient: Eggplant Portion: 1 bowl (approx. 350g) Nutrition: 388 Calories, 25g Carbs, 6g Protein, 31g Fat, 12g Fiber
Recipe: Roast 1 medium eggplant (approx. 400g) over a flame or in the oven until the skin is charred and flesh is soft. Peel and mash the flesh. In a pan, heat 2 tablespoons of butter. Sauté 1/2 chopped onion, 1 chopped tomato, garlic, and ginger. Add the mashed eggplant and cook for 10 minutes.

Dinner: Garlic Butter Broccoli (7:30 PM)

Main Ingredient: Broccoli Portion: 1 serving (approx. 265g) Nutrition: 444 Calories, 15g Carbs, 9g Protein, 41g Fat, 7g Fiber
Recipe: Steam 250g of broccoli florets until tender-crisp. In a pan, melt 3 tablespoons of butter with 2 minced cloves of garlic. Toss the steamed broccoli in the garlic butter. Garnish with 1 tablespoon of sesame seeds.

Snack: Blueberries with Almonds (4:00 PM)

Main Ingredient: Blueberry Portion: 1 small bowl (105g) Nutrition: 216 Calories, 16g Carbs, 7g Protein, 15g Fat, 5g Fiber
Recipe: Combine 75g of fresh blueberries with 30g of whole almonds.
Day 3

Daily Summary

Total Calories: 1642 kcal

Nutrition: Carbs: 48g | Protein: 55g | Fat: 142g | Fiber: 22g

Pre-Meal Appetizer: Sliced Tomatoes with Pine Nuts (12:30 PM)

Main Ingredient: Tomatoes Portion: 1 plate Nutrition: 130 Calories, 10g Carbs, 4g Protein, 9g Fat, 3g Fiber
Recipe: Slice 2 medium tomatoes and arrange on a plate. Sprinkle with 15g of toasted pine nuts.

Breakfast: Sautéed Chard with Fried Eggs (8:00 AM)

Main Ingredient: Chard Portion: 1 serving Nutrition: 328 Calories, 6g Carbs, 16g Protein, 28g Fat, 3g Fiber
Recipe: In a pan, heat 1 tablespoon of butter and sauté 150g of chopped chard with 1 clove of garlic until wilted. In a separate pan, fry 2 large eggs in 1 tablespoon of butter. Serve the eggs over the chard.

Lunch: Cabbage and Onion Stir-fry (Sabji) (1:00 PM)

Main Ingredient: Cabbage Portion: 1 large bowl (approx. 350g) Nutrition: 489 Calories, 22g Carbs, 6g Protein, 43g Fat, 9g Fiber
Recipe: Shred 250g of cabbage. In a wok or pan, heat 3 tablespoons of butter. Sauté 1 sliced onion, 1 clove of garlic, and 1/2 inch of grated ginger. Add the cabbage and stir-fry for 10-15 minutes until tender. Garnish with 15g of crushed pecans.

Dinner: Wilted Kale with Garlic and Hazelnuts (7:30 PM)

Main Ingredient: Kale Portion: 1 bowl (approx. 230g) Nutrition: 455 Calories, 18g Carbs, 12g Protein, 40g Fat, 5g Fiber
Recipe: In a large pan, melt 2 tablespoons of butter. Add 2 cloves of minced garlic and sauté for 30 seconds. Add 200g of chopped kale and cook until wilted. Stir in 30g of chopped hazelnuts.

Snack: Handful of Almonds (4:00 PM)

Main Ingredient: Almonds Portion: 40g Nutrition: 240 Calories, 9g Carbs, 9g Protein, 21g Fat, 5g Fiber
Recipe: Enjoy 40g of whole, raw almonds.
Day 4

Daily Summary

Total Calories: 1675 kcal

Nutrition: Carbs: 45g | Protein: 58g | Fat: 145g | Fiber: 26g

Pre-Meal Appetizer: Arugula with Pecans (12:30 PM)

Main Ingredient: Arugula Portion: 1 small bowl (approx. 75g) Nutrition: 255 Calories, 4g Carbs, 3g Protein, 26g Fat, 3g Fiber
Recipe: Toss 50g of arugula with 20g of chopped pecans and a dressing of 1 tablespoon of melted butter.

Breakfast: Three-Egg Omelette (8:00 AM)

Main Ingredient: Eggs Portion: 1 omelette Nutrition: 427 Calories, 2g Carbs, 19g Protein, 38g Fat, 0g Fiber
Recipe: Whisk 3 large eggs. Melt 2 tablespoons of butter in a pan, pour in the eggs, and cook until set. Fold and serve.

Lunch: Mushroom and Onion Sabji (1:00 PM)

Main Ingredient: Mushrooms Portion: 1 bowl (approx. 300g) Nutrition: 523 Calories, 16g Carbs, 11g Protein, 49g Fat, 6g Fiber
Recipe: Slice 200g of mushrooms and 1 medium onion. In a pan, heat 3 tablespoons of butter. Sauté the onion, 2 cloves of garlic, and ginger until soft. Add the mushrooms and cook until browned. Stir in 20g of crushed walnuts for texture.

Dinner: Braised Artichoke Hearts in Butter (7:30 PM)

Main Ingredient: Artichoke Portion: 1 serving (approx. 200g) Nutrition: 280 Calories, 20g Carbs, 7g Protein, 20g Fat, 11g Fiber
Recipe: Take 200g of canned or cooked artichoke hearts, drained. In a pan, melt 2 tablespoons of butter. Add the artichoke hearts and 2 cloves of minced garlic. Cook gently for 10 minutes until tender and infused with garlic flavor.

Snack: Handful of Walnuts (4:00 PM)

Main Ingredient: Walnuts Portion: 30g Nutrition: 190 Calories, 4g Carbs, 5g Protein, 20g Fat, 2g Fiber
Recipe: Enjoy 30g of whole, raw walnuts.
Day 5

Daily Summary

Total Calories: 1661 kcal

Nutrition: Carbs: 60g | Protein: 50g | Fat: 145g | Fiber: 29g

Pre-Meal Appetizer: Quick Cabbage Slaw (12:30 PM)

Main Ingredient: Cabbage Portion: 1 small bowl (approx. 110g) Nutrition: 182 Calories, 8g Carbs, 3g Protein, 16g Fat, 4g Fiber
Recipe: Finely shred 100g of cabbage. Toss with 1 tablespoon of melted butter and 10g of sesame seeds.

Breakfast: Spinach and Egg Scramble (8:00 AM)

Main Ingredient: Spinach Portion: 1 serving Nutrition: 328 Calories, 4g Carbs, 17g Protein, 28g Fat, 2g Fiber
Recipe: In a pan with 1 tablespoon of butter, sauté 100g of spinach and 1 clove of garlic until wilted. Push to one side. Add another tablespoon of butter and scramble 2 large eggs in the pan. Mix everything together.

Lunch: Cauliflower 'Aloo Gobi' Style (1:00 PM)

Main Ingredient: Cauliflower Portion: 1 large bowl (approx. 350g) Nutrition: 445 Calories, 22g Carbs, 7g Protein, 38g Fat, 9g Fiber
Recipe: Cut 250g of cauliflower into florets. In a pan, heat 3 tablespoons of butter. Sauté 1/2 chopped onion, garlic, and ginger. Add the cauliflower and 1 chopped tomato. Cook, covered, for 15-20 minutes until cauliflower is tender.

Dinner: Stuffed Eggplant with Nuts (7:30 PM)

Main Ingredient: Eggplant Portion: 1 medium eggplant Nutrition: 490 Calories, 22g Carbs, 11g Protein, 42g Fat, 12g Fiber
Recipe: Cut 1 medium eggplant (400g) in half lengthwise. Score the flesh. Brush with 1 tbsp of melted butter and roast until soft. Scoop out the flesh, leaving a shell. Mash the flesh with garlic, 1 tbsp butter, and 30g of crushed almonds. Refill the eggplant shells and bake for 10 more minutes.

Snack: Handful of Pecans (4:00 PM)

Main Ingredient: Pecans Portion: 30g Nutrition: 216 Calories, 4g Carbs, 3g Protein, 22g Fat, 3g Fiber
Recipe: Enjoy 30g of whole, raw pecans.
Day 6

Daily Summary

Total Calories: 1644 kcal

Nutrition: Carbs: 49g | Protein: 54g | Fat: 143g | Fiber: 23g

Pre-Meal Appetizer: Lettuce Cups with Almonds (12:30 PM)

Main Ingredient: Lettuce Portion: 4 lettuce cups Nutrition: 178 Calories, 7g Carbs, 5g Protein, 16g Fat, 4g Fiber
Recipe: Use 4 large lettuce leaves as cups. Fill with a mixture of 20g of chopped almonds and 1/4 chopped onion, tossed with 1 teaspoon of melted butter.

Breakfast: Fried Eggs with Sautéed Tomatoes (8:00 AM)

Main Ingredient: Eggs Portion: 1 serving Nutrition: 317 Calories, 6g Carbs, 14g Protein, 27g Fat, 1g Fiber
Recipe: In a pan, melt 1 tablespoon of butter and sauté 1 sliced medium tomato with garlic until soft. Push to the side. Add another tablespoon of butter and fry 2 large eggs to your liking. Serve together.

Lunch: Broccoli and Almond Stir-Fry (1:00 PM)

Main Ingredient: Broccoli Portion: 1 bowl (approx. 280g) Nutrition: 541 Calories, 19g Carbs, 14g Protein, 49g Fat, 10g Fiber
Recipe: Chop 250g of broccoli. In a pan, heat 3 tablespoons of butter. Sauté garlic and ginger, then add the broccoli and stir-fry until tender-crisp. Stir in 30g of slivered almonds.

Dinner: Garlic Chard with Pine Nuts (7:30 PM)

Main Ingredient: Chard Portion: 1 bowl (approx. 220g) Nutrition: 392 Calories, 9g Carbs, 9g Protein, 37g Fat, 4g Fiber
Recipe: Sauté 200g of chopped chard with 2 cloves of garlic in 2 tablespoons of butter until wilted. Top with 20g of toasted pine nuts.

Snack: Strawberries with Walnuts (4:00 PM)

Main Ingredient: Strawberry Portion: 1 small bowl (120g) Nutrition: 216 Calories, 10g Carbs, 4g Protein, 18g Fat, 4g Fiber
Recipe: Combine 100g of sliced strawberries with 20g of crushed walnuts.
Day 7

Daily Summary

Total Calories: 1682 kcal

Nutrition: Carbs: 55g | Protein: 54g | Fat: 146g | Fiber: 24g

Pre-Meal Appetizer: Tomato and Onion Salad (12:30 PM)

Main Ingredient: Tomatoes Portion: 1 small bowl Nutrition: 150 Calories, 8g Carbs, 2g Protein, 13g Fat, 2g Fiber
Recipe: Slice 1 large tomato and 1/4 onion. Drizzle with 1 tablespoon of melted butter.

Breakfast: Kale and Egg Scramble (8:00 AM)

Main Ingredient: Kale Portion: 1 serving Nutrition: 354 Calories, 8g Carbs, 18g Protein, 30g Fat, 2g Fiber
Recipe: Finely chop 100g of kale. In a pan, melt 1 tablespoon of butter and sauté the kale with garlic until tender. Whisk 2 large eggs with another tablespoon of butter and scramble with the kale.

Lunch: Cabbage 'Poriyal' (Stir-fry) (1:00 PM)

Main Ingredient: Cabbage Portion: 1 bowl (approx. 280g) Nutrition: 530 Calories, 20g Carbs, 8g Protein, 48g Fat, 9g Fiber
Recipe: Shred 250g of cabbage. Heat 3 tablespoons of butter in a pan. Sauté garlic and ginger. Add the cabbage and cook until soft. Stir in 30g of chopped hazelnuts for crunch.

Dinner: Mushroom 'Masala' with Arugula (7:30 PM)

Main Ingredient: Mushrooms Portion: 1 bowl (approx. 300g) Nutrition: 378 Calories, 14g Carbs, 10g Protein, 33g Fat, 5g Fiber
Recipe: In a pan with 2 tablespoons of butter, sauté 1/2 chopped onion, garlic, and ginger. Add 200g of sliced mushrooms and cook until browned. Stir in 50g of fresh arugula at the end until just wilted.

Snack: Blueberries with Pecans (4:00 PM)

Main Ingredient: Blueberry Portion: 1 small bowl (100g) Nutrition: 270 Calories, 14g Carbs, 3g Protein, 23g Fat, 4g Fiber
Recipe: Combine 75g of fresh blueberries with 25g of pecans.
