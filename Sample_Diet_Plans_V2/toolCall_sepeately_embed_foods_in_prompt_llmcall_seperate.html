Pre-Dinner Lettuce Salad (06:45 PM)
Portion: 1 small bowl
Nutrition: 25 kcal, Carbs: 2.3g, Protein: 1.0g, Fat: 1.6g, Fiber: 1.1g
Recipe: Toss 1 cup of shredded lettuce with 1 tsp of toasted sesame seeds. Season with salt and pepper.

Main Meals

Breakfast: Palak Anda Bhurji (Spinach Scrambled Eggs) (08:00 AM)
Portion: 1 serving
Nutrition: 282 kcal, Carbs: 3.5g, Protein: 13.3g, Fat: 24.0g, Fiber: 1.1g
Recipe: Heat 1 tbsp of ghee in a pan. Sauté 1/4 chopped onion and 1 minced garlic clove. Add 1 cup of chopped spinach and cook until wilted. Pour in 2 whisked eggs, season with salt and turmeric, and scramble until cooked. Serve hot.
Lunch: Pan-Seared Masala Fish with Cabbage Stir-fry (01:00 PM)
Portion: 1 fillet and 1 cup of cabbage
Nutrition: 452 kcal, Carbs: 10.0g, Protein: 31.8g, Fat: 32.1g, Fiber: 3.5g
Recipe: Marinate a 150g fish fillet (Kingfish) with ginger-garlic paste, turmeric, and salt. Pan-sear in 1/2 tbsp of ghee until cooked. In another pan, heat 1/2 tbsp ghee, splutter mustard seeds (from sesame seeds family, assumed allowed), add 1.5 cups of shredded cabbage, turmeric, and salt. Stir-fry until tender.
Dinner: Keema Baingan Bharta (Minced Lamb with Eggplant Mash) (07:00 PM)
Portion: 1.5 cups
Nutrition: 587 kcal, Carbs: 25.0g, Protein: 41.5g, Fat: 36.5g, Fiber: 9.5g
Recipe: Roast 1 large eggplant over a flame until skin is charred. Cool, peel, and mash. In a pan, heat 1 tbsp ghee, sauté 1/2 chopped onion, ginger-garlic paste. Add 150g minced lamb and cook until browned. Add 1 chopped tomato, spices (turmeric, cumin, coriander powder), and the mashed eggplant. Cook for 10-15 minutes.

Nutrition: 305 kcal, Carbs: 6.2g, Protein: 15.1g, Fat: 25.0g, Fiber: 1.5g

Lunch: Lamb Chops with Masala Broccoli (01:00 PM)
Portion: 2 chops and 1 cup broccoli
Nutrition: 520 kcal, Carbs: 8.5g, Protein: 40.0g, Fat: 36.0g, Fiber: 3.5g
Recipe: Marinate 2 lamb chops (total 150g meat) in ginger-garlic paste, garam masala, and salt. Pan-sear in 1 tbsp ghee until cooked. Steam 1 cup of broccoli florets. In the same pan, toss the steamed broccoli with a pinch of turmeric and salt.
Dinner: Tandoori-Style Fish with Cauliflower Rice (07:00 PM)
Portion: 1 serving
Nutrition: 488 kcal, Carbs: 15.0g, Protein: 41.0g, Fat: 30.3g, Fiber: 7.0g
Recipe: Marinate 150g fish chunks in a paste of ginger, garlic, tomato paste, and tandoori masala spices. Bake or pan-fry until cooked. For cauliflower rice, grate 1.5 cups of cauliflower florets. Sauté in 1 tbsp ghee with cumin seeds and salt until tender.

Main Meals

Breakfast: Indian-Style Shakshuka (08:30 AM)
Portion: 1 serving
Nutrition: 320 kcal, Carbs: 12.0g, Protein: 14.5g, Fat: 25.0g, Fiber: 3.0g
Recipe: Heat 1 tbsp ghee. Sauté 1/2 chopped onion, garlic, and ginger. Add 1 large chopped tomato, turmeric, cumin powder, and salt. Cook into a thick gravy. Make two wells and crack 2 eggs into them. Cover and cook until eggs are set.
Lunch: Palak Fish (Spinach Fish Curry) (01:00 PM)
Portion: 1.5 cups
Nutrition: 478 kcal, Carbs: 12.8g, Protein: 34.0g, Fat: 33.3g, Fiber: 5.2g
Recipe: Blanch 2 cups of spinach and blend into a puree. In a pan with 1 tbsp ghee, sauté onion-ginger-garlic paste. Add the spinach puree, turmeric, and garam masala. Add 150g of fish chunks and simmer until the fish is cooked through.
Dinner: Lamb and Cabbage Stir-Fry (Patta Gobi Gosht) (07:00 PM)
Portion: 2 cups
Nutrition: 550 kcal, Carbs: 10.0g, Protein: 40.0g, Fat: 39.0g, Fiber: 5.0g
Recipe: In a pressure cooker or pot with 1 tbsp ghee, brown 150g lamb pieces with ginger-garlic paste. Add turmeric, salt, and a little water and cook until tender. Add 2 cups of shredded cabbage and cook until the cabbage is soft and integrated with the lamb.

Simmer for 5 minutes. Gently place 150g of fish chunks into the curry and cook for 7-10 minutes until done.
