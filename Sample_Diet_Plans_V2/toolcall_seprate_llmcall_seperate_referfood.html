7-Day LCHF Indian Meal Plan

A personalized 7-day Low-Carb High-Fat (LCHF) Indian meal plan for a 40-year-old with Type 2 Diabetes and a BMI of 20.0 for weight maintenance. This plan excludes chicken and ensures no primary ingredient is repeated within the same day.

Meal Details Recipe
Breakfast (08:00 AM)
Spinach and Masala Omelette
Portion: 1 serving
 Ingredients:
  • 2 Large Eggs
  • 1 tbsp Ghee
  • 25g Chopped Onion
  • 50g Chopped Spinach
  • Turmeric, Red Chili Powder, Salt to taste
Nutrition:
  • Calories: 313
  • Carbs: 5.4g
  • Protein: 13.8g
  • Fat: 25.3g
  1. Whisk eggs with spices and salt.
  2. Heat ghee in a pan, sauté the chopped onion until translucent.
  3. Add spinach and cook until wilted.
  4. Pour the egg mixture over the vegetables.
  5. Cook on low heat until the omelette is set. Fold and serve hot.
Lunch (01:00 PM)
Tawa Fish Fry with Green Salad
Portion: 150g fish + 1 cup salad
 Ingredients:
  • 150g Mackerel Fish Fillet
  • 1 tsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • Spices: Turmeric, Red Chili Powder, Salt
  • 2 tbsp Coconut Oil
  • 100g Mixed Greens
  • 1 tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • 1 tsp Lemon Juice
Nutrition:
  • Calories: 662
  • Carbs: 6.0g
  • Protein: 37.0g
  • Fat: 54.0g
  1. Marinate fish with ginger-garlic paste and spices for 15 minutes.
  2. Heat coconut oil in a tawa and pan-fry the fish until golden.
  3. Toss mixed greens with olive oil and lemon juice.
  4. Serve hot fish with the salad.
Dinner (07:30 PM)
Paneer Bhurji with Bell Peppers
Portion: 1.5 cups
 Ingredients:
  • 150g Paneer (crumbled)
  • 2 tbsp Ghee
  • 50g Chopped Onion
  • 100g Chopped Bell Pepper
  • 50g Chopped Tomato
  • Spices: Turmeric, Cumin, Garam Masala
Nutrition:
  • Calories: 585
  • Carbs: 15.2g
  • Protein: 28.1g
  • Fat: 47.3g
  1. Heat ghee, add cumin seeds.
  2. Sauté onions, then add tomatoes and cook until soft.
  3. Add bell peppers and cook for 3-4 minutes.
  4. Add spices, salt, and crumbled paneer. Cook for 5-7 minutes.
Snack (04:30 PM)
Walnuts
Portion: 28g
 Ingredients:
  • 28g Raw Walnuts
Nutrition:
  • Calories: 185
  • Carbs: 3.3g
  • Protein: 19.5g
  • Fat: 8.0g

Consume a handful of raw walnuts.

MealDetailsRecipe
Breakfast (08:30 AM)
Masala Cauliflower 'Upma'
Portion: 1.5 cups
 Ingredients:
  • 200g Grated Cauliflower
  • 2 tbsp Coconut Oil
  • 1 tsp Mustard Seeds
  • 50g Chopped Onion
  • 1 Green Chili
  • Turmeric Powder, Salt to taste
  • 1 tsp Lemon Juice
Nutrition:
  • Calories: 325
  • Carbs: 14.5g
  • Protein: 7.5g
  • Fat: 26.5g
  1. Heat oil, add mustard seeds, onion, and chili. Sauté.
  2. Add grated cauliflower, turmeric, and salt.
  3. Cover and cook on low heat for 7-10 minutes.
  4. Squeeze lemon juice before serving.
Lunch (01:00 PM)
Bhindi Gosht (Mutton with Okra)
Portion: 1 bowl
 Ingredients:
  • 150g Boneless Mutton
  • 150g Okra (Bhindi)
  • 3 tbsp Ghee
  • 75g Sliced Onion
  • 1 tbsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • 50g Tomato Puree
  • Spices: Coriander, Cumin, Turmeric, Garam Masala
Nutrition:
  • Calories: 788
  • Carbs: 10.0g
  • Protein: 40.5g
  • Fat: 66.0g
  1. In a pressure cooker, sauté onions in ghee.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste, tomato puree, and spices.
  3. Add mutton and pressure cook until tender.
  4. Add okra and cook without the lid until tender.
Dinner (07:30 PM)
Cabbage and Tofu Stir-fry
Portion: 1.5 cups
 Ingredients:
  • 250g Firm Tofu
  • 150g Shredded Cabbage
  • 2 tbsp Sesame Oil
  • 1 tbsp Soy Sauce
  • 2 cloves Chopped Garlic
  • White Pepper to taste
Nutrition:
  • Calories: 597
  • Carbs: 7.9g
  • Protein: 48.3g
  • Fat: 40.6g
  1. Press and cube tofu, then fry until golden.
  2. In the same pan, stir-fry garlic.
  3. Add cabbage and stir-fry until tender-crisp.
  4. Return tofu to the pan, add soy sauce and pepper. Toss and serve.
Snack (04:30 PM)
Roasted Pumpkin Seeds
Portion: 30g
 Ingredients:
  • 30g Pumpkin Seeds
  • A pinch of Salt
Nutrition:
  • Calories: 155
  • Carbs: 1.0g
  • Protein: 8.0g
  • Fat: 12.0g

Lightly dry roast the pumpkin seeds in a pan with a pinch of salt until crisp.
