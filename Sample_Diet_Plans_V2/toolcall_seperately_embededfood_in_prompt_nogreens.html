7-Day Meal Plan

Dietary Information

Allowed Foods: Almonds, Artichoke, Arugula, Broccoli, Cabbage, Cauliflower, Chard, Eggplant, Eggs, Fish, Garlic, Ginger, Hazelnuts, Kale, Lettuce, Macadamia nuts, Meat, Mushrooms, Onion, Pecans, Pine nuts, Poultry, Sesame seeds, Spinach, Strawberry, Tomatoes, Walnuts

Food Restrictions: Greens

Allergens: Dairy

Day 1

Daily Summary

Total Calories: 1502 kcal
Nutrition: 29g Net Carbs | 105g Protein | 106g Fat | 13g Fiber

Pre-Meal Appetizer

Tomato and Walnut Salad (12:45 PM)
Portion: 1 serving
Nutrition: 120 kcal, 3g Net Carbs, 3g Protein, 11g Fat, 2g Fiber
Recipe: 1. Dice one medium tomato. 2. Roughly chop 15g of walnuts. 3. Combine and season with a pinch of salt and black pepper.

Breakfast

Masala Scrambled Eggs (Anda Bhurji) (8:00 AM)
Portion: 3 eggs
Nutrition: 385 kcal, 6g Net Carbs, 20g Protein, 31g Fat, 1g Fiber
Recipe: 1. Heat 1 tbsp of sesame oil in a pan. 2. Sauté 1/4 finely chopped onion, 1 tsp minced ginger, and 1 tsp minced garlic until fragrant. 3. Add 1/2 chopped tomato and cook until soft. 4. Add 1/2 tsp turmeric powder, 1/2 tsp red chili powder, and salt. 5. Whisk 3 large eggs and pour into the pan. 6. Scramble until cooked to your liking.

Lunch

Spiced Pan-Seared Fish with Broccoli (1:00 PM)
Portion: 150g fish fillet, 150g broccoli
Nutrition: 402 kcal, 5g Net Carbs, 40g Protein, 24g Fat, 5g Fiber
Recipe: 1. Marinate a 150g fillet of fish (e.g., salmon or cod) with 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste, 1/2 tsp turmeric, 1/2 tsp garam masala, and salt. 2. Heat 1 tbsp of sesame oil in a pan and sear the fish for 3-4 minutes per side until cooked through. 3. In a separate pot, steam 150g of broccoli florets until tender-crisp.

Dinner

Chicken and Cauliflower Korma (7:30 PM)
Portion: 1 serving
Nutrition: 455 kcal, 10g Net Carbs, 36g Protein, 30g Fat, 5g Fiber
Recipe: 1. Blend 30g of macadamia nuts with 1/4 cup of water to make a cream. 2. Heat 1 tbsp sesame oil. Sauté 1/4 chopped onion, 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste. 3. Add 150g of diced poultry (chicken thigh) and brown it. 4. Stir in spices: 1/2 tsp coriander powder, 1/2 tsp cumin powder, 1/4 tsp turmeric, and salt. 5. Add 1/2 cup chopped tomatoes and cook until pulpy. 6. Add 150g cauliflower florets and the nut cream. Simmer for 10-15 minutes until chicken is cooked and gravy has thickened.

Snack

Strawberries (4:00 PM)
Portion: 100g
Nutrition: 140 kcal, 5g Net Carbs, 6g Protein, 11g Fat, 0g Fiber
Recipe: Enjoy a bowl of fresh strawberries.
Day 2

Daily Summary

Total Calories: 1515 kcal
Nutrition: 26g Net Carbs | 98g Protein | 113g Fat | 12g Fiber

Pre-Meal Appetizer

Roasted Almonds (12:45 PM)
Portion: 15g
Nutrition: 87 kcal, 1g Net Carbs, 3g Protein, 8g Fat, 2g Fiber
Recipe: 1. Lightly toast 15g of almonds in a dry pan until fragrant.

Breakfast

Spiced Poultry Patties (8:00 AM)
Portion: 2 patties
Nutrition: 350 kcal, 1g Net Carbs, 32g Protein, 24g Fat, 0g Fiber
Recipe: 1. Mix 150g of minced poultry (chicken) with 1/2 tsp ginger paste, 1/2 tsp garlic paste, 1/4 tsp garam masala, and salt. 2. Form into two small patties. 3. Pan-fry in 1 tbsp of sesame oil until cooked through and golden brown.

Lunch

Meat (Lamb) and Cabbage Bhaji (1:00 PM)
Portion: 1 serving
Nutrition: 488 kcal, 8g Net Carbs, 35g Protein, 36g Fat, 4g Fiber
Recipe: 1. Heat 1 tbsp sesame oil. Sauté 1/4 chopped onion. 2. Add 150g of cubed meat (lamb) and brown on all sides. 3. Add 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste, 1/2 tsp turmeric, 1 tsp coriander powder, and salt. 4. Add 1/4 cup water, cover, and cook until the meat is tender (approx. 30-40 mins). 5. Add 150g of shredded cabbage and cook for another 5-7 minutes until cabbage is tender.

Dinner

Mushroom Masala Curry (7:30 PM)
Portion: 1 serving
Nutrition: 420 kcal, 11g Net Carbs, 13g Protein, 36g Fat, 4g Fiber
Recipe: 1. Make a paste of 1 large tomato and 20g of walnuts. 2. Heat 1.5 tbsp of sesame oil. Sauté 1/4 chopped onion until golden. 3. Add 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute. 4. Add the tomato-walnut paste, 1/2 tsp turmeric, 1 tsp red chili powder, and salt. Cook until oil separates. 5. Add 200g of sliced mushrooms and 1/2 cup of water. Simmer for 10 minutes.

Snack

Hard-Boiled Egg (4:00 PM)
Portion: 1 large egg
Nutrition: 170 kcal, 5g Net Carbs, 15g Protein, 9g Fat, 2g Fiber
Recipe: 1. Boil one large egg for 10 minutes. 2. Peel and season with salt and pepper.
Day 3

Daily Summary

Total Calories: 1493 kcal
Nutrition: 31g Net Carbs | 92g Protein | 111g Fat | 18g Fiber

Pre-Meal Appetizer

Sliced Tomatoes with Sesame Seeds (12:45 PM)
Portion: 1 medium tomato
Nutrition: 40 kcal, 3g Net Carbs, 2g Protein, 2g Fat, 1g Fiber
Recipe: 1. Slice one medium tomato. 2. Sprinkle with 1 tsp of toasted sesame seeds, salt, and pepper.

Breakfast

Mushroom and Onion Omelette (8:00 AM)
Portion: 3-egg omelette
Nutrition: 395 kcal, 5g Net Carbs, 22g Protein, 32g Fat, 1g Fiber
Recipe: 1. Sauté 50g sliced mushrooms and 1/4 chopped onion in 1 tbsp of sesame oil until soft. 2. Whisk 3 large eggs with salt and pepper. 3. Pour eggs over the vegetables and cook until set.

Lunch

Fish Curry with Almond Cream (1:00 PM)
Portion: 1 serving
Nutrition: 438 kcal, 7g Net Carbs, 33g Protein, 31g Fat, 3g Fiber
Recipe: 1. Blend 20g almonds with water to make a paste. 2. Heat 1 tbsp sesame oil, sauté 1/4 onion, 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste. 3. Add 1/2 chopped tomato, 1/2 tsp turmeric, 1/2 tsp cumin powder. Cook until soft. 4. Add the almond paste and 1/2 cup water. Simmer for 5 mins. 5. Gently add 150g of firm white fish chunks (like cod) and cook for 5-7 minutes until fish is opaque.

Dinner

Stuffed Eggplant (Bharwa Baingan) (7:30 PM)
Portion: 2 stuffed eggplants
Nutrition: 450 kcal, 12g Net Carbs, 29g Protein, 35g Fat, 11g Fiber
Recipe: 1. Slit 2 medium eggplants (total 250g). 2. Mix 2 tbsp powdered pecans, 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste, 1 tsp coriander powder, 1/2 tsp turmeric, salt, and 1 tbsp sesame oil into a paste. 3. Stuff this paste into the eggplants. 4. In a pan, heat 1.5 tbsp sesame oil and place the stuffed eggplants. 5. Add 1/4 cup water, cover, and cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes, turning occasionally, until eggplant is tender.

Snack

Handful of Walnuts (4:00 PM)
Portion: 20g
Nutrition: 170 kcal, 4g Net Carbs, 6g Protein, 11g Fat, 2g Fiber
Recipe: A handful of raw walnuts.
Day 4

Daily Summary

Total Calories: 1523 kcal
Nutrition: 30g Net Carbs | 102g Protein | 111g Fat | 14g Fiber

Pre-Meal Appetizer

Artichoke Hearts in Oil (12:45 PM)
Portion: 50g
Nutrition: 50 kcal, 2g Net Carbs, 1g Protein, 4g Fat, 3g Fiber
Recipe: 1. Take 50g of canned artichoke hearts (in oil, drained). 2. Season with a pinch of black pepper.

Breakfast

Meat Keema with Eggs (8:00 AM)
Portion: 1 serving
Nutrition: 480 kcal, 7g Net Carbs, 35g Protein, 35g Fat, 2g Fiber
Recipe: 1. Heat 1 tbsp sesame oil. Sauté 1/4 chopped onion, 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste. 2. Add 100g minced meat (lamb/goat) and cook until browned. 3. Add 1/2 chopped tomato, 1/2 tsp turmeric, 1/2 tsp garam masala, and salt. Cook for 10 mins. 4. Create two wells in the keema and crack two large eggs into them. 5. Cover and cook until eggs are set to your liking.

Lunch

Poultry (Chicken) Tikka Skewers (1:00 PM)
Portion: 2 skewers
Nutrition: 353 kcal, 6g Net Carbs, 38g Protein, 19g Fat, 2g Fiber
Recipe: 1. Cube 150g of poultry (chicken breast). Marinate in 1 tbsp sesame oil, 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste, 1 tsp tandoori masala (or mix of cumin, coriander, chili powder), and salt for 30 mins. 2. Thread chicken onto skewers, alternating with chunks of onion and tomato. 3. Grill or pan-sear until cooked through.

Dinner

Cauliflower 'Rice' with Sautéed Mushrooms (7:30 PM)
Portion: 1 large bowl
Nutrition: 470 kcal, 12g Net Carbs, 22g Protein, 38g Fat, 7g Fiber
Recipe: 1. Grate 200g of cauliflower to a 'rice' consistency. 2. Heat 1 tbsp sesame oil. Sauté 1/2 tsp cumin seeds, 1/4 chopped onion. 3. Add 100g sliced mushrooms and cook until browned. 4. Add the cauliflower 'rice', 1/2 tsp turmeric, and salt. Stir-fry for 5-7 minutes until tender. 5. Garnish with 15g toasted pine nuts.

Snack

Macadamia Nuts (4:00 PM)
Portion: 20g
Nutrition: 170 kcal, 3g Net Carbs, 6g Protein, 15g Fat, 0g Fiber
Recipe: A small handful of raw macadamia nuts.
Day 5

Daily Summary

Total Calories: 1498 kcal
Nutrition: 28g Net Carbs | 95g Protein | 112g Fat | 15g Fiber

Pre-Meal Appetizer

Strawberry Slices (12:45 PM)
Portion: 50g
Nutrition: 16 kcal, 3g Net Carbs, 0g Protein, 0g Fat, 1g Fiber
Recipe: 1. Slice 50g of fresh strawberries.

Breakfast

Fried Eggs with Sautéed Tomatoes (8:00 AM)
Portion: 2 large eggs, 1 tomato
Nutrition: 320 kcal, 6g Net Carbs, 14g Protein, 27g Fat, 2g Fiber
Recipe: 1. Heat 1 tbsp of sesame oil in a pan. 2. Add 1 sliced tomato and sauté until soft. 3. Push tomatoes to one side and fry 2 large eggs in the same pan until the whites are set and yolks are runny.

Lunch

Baked Fish with Garlic and Herbs (1:00 PM)
Portion: 150g fish, 100g broccoli
Nutrition: 482 kcal, 5g Net Carbs, 36g Protein, 36g Fat, 4g Fiber
Recipe: 1. Preheat oven to 200°C (400°F). 2. Place a 150g fish fillet (e.g., mackerel) on a piece of parchment paper. 3. Top with 2 cloves of minced garlic, 1 tbsp of sesame oil, salt, and pepper. 4. Fold the parchment to create a packet and bake for 15-20 minutes. 5. Serve with a side of 100g steamed broccoli.

Dinner

Spicy Cabbage with Minced Poultry (7:30 PM)
Portion: 1 serving
Nutrition: 510 kcal, 10g Net Carbs, 39g Protein, 35g Fat, 6g Fiber
Recipe: 1. Heat 1.5 tbsp sesame oil. Sauté 1/4 chopped onion and 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste. 2. Add 150g of minced poultry (chicken) and cook until browned. 3. Add spices: 1/2 tsp turmeric, 1 tsp chili powder, 1 tsp coriander powder. 4. Add 200g of shredded cabbage and salt. Stir-fry on high heat for 5-7 minutes until cabbage is cooked but still has a slight crunch.

Snack

Pecans (4:00 PM)
Portion: 20g
Nutrition: 170 kcal, 4g Net Carbs, 6g Protein, 14g Fat, 2g Fiber
Recipe: A small handful of raw pecans.
Day 6

Daily Summary

Total Calories: 1512 kcal
Nutrition: 25g Net Carbs | 108g Protein | 108g Fat | 11g Fiber

Pre-Meal Appetizer

Simple Tomato Soup Shot (12:45 PM)
Portion: 1 small cup (~100ml)
Nutrition: 15 kcal, 2g Net Carbs, 1g Protein, 0g Fat, 1g Fiber
Recipe: 1. Blend 1/2 a large tomato with 2-3 tbsp of hot water, a pinch of salt, and black pepper until smooth. 2. Serve warm in a small cup.

Breakfast

Scrambled Eggs with Onions (8:00 AM)
Portion: 3 eggs
Nutrition: 375 kcal, 4g Net Carbs, 20g Protein, 31g Fat, 1g Fiber
Recipe: 1. Heat 1 tbsp sesame oil. Sauté 1/4 finely chopped onion until soft. 2. Whisk 3 large eggs with salt and pepper, add to the pan, and scramble until cooked.

Lunch

Meat (Lamb) Seekh Kebabs (1:00 PM)
Portion: 2 large kebabs
Nutrition: 482 kcal, 6g Net Carbs, 38g Protein, 34g Fat, 3g Fiber
Recipe: 1. Mix 150g minced meat (lamb) with 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste, 1/4 chopped onion, 1/2 tsp garam masala, salt, and 1/2 tsp chili powder. 2. Form the mixture around skewers. 3. Pan-fry with 1 tbsp sesame oil or grill until cooked through. Serve with a side of 100g steamed cauliflower.

Dinner

Poultry and Broccoli Stir-fry (7:30 PM)
Portion: 1 serving
Nutrition: 470 kcal, 9g Net Carbs, 43g Protein, 29g Fat, 5g Fiber
Recipe: 1. Cut 150g poultry (chicken breast) into strips. 2. Heat 1.5 tbsp sesame oil in a wok. Add 1 tsp minced ginger and 1 tsp minced garlic. 3. Add the chicken and stir-fry until almost cooked. 4. Add 150g of broccoli florets and stir-fry for 4-5 minutes. 5. Add 1 tbsp of water, salt, and pepper. Cover and steam for 2 minutes until broccoli is tender-crisp.

Snack

Toasted Sesame Seeds (4:00 PM)
Portion: 2 tbsp (18g)
Nutrition: 170 kcal, 4g Net Carbs, 6g Protein, 14g Fat, 1g Fiber
Recipe: Toast 2 tablespoons of sesame seeds in a dry pan until lightly golden and fragrant. Eat as is or with a pinch of salt.
Day 7

Daily Summary

Total Calories: 1506 kcal
Nutrition: 29g Net Carbs | 99g Protein | 110g Fat | 16g Fiber

Pre-Meal Appetizer

Walnuts and Strawberries (12:45 PM)
Portion: 1 small bowl
Nutrition: 75 kcal, 2g Net Carbs, 2g Protein, 7g Fat, 2g Fiber
Recipe: 1. A small mix of 10g walnuts and 30g sliced strawberries.

Breakfast

Cauliflower Upma with Nuts (8:00 AM)
Portion: 1 bowl
Nutrition: 356 kcal, 9g Net Carbs, 10g Protein, 31g Fat, 6g Fiber
Recipe: 1. Grate 150g of cauliflower. 2. Heat 1.5 tbsp sesame oil. Sauté 1/4 chopped onion, 1 tsp ginger. 3. Add the grated cauliflower, 1/2 tsp turmeric, and salt. Cook for 5-7 minutes. 4. Stir in 20g of crushed almonds for crunch.

Lunch

Pan-Seared Fish with Tomato-Onion Masala (1:00 PM)
Portion: 150g fish fillet
Nutrition: 380 kcal, 6g Net Carbs, 36g Protein, 23g Fat, 2g Fiber
Recipe: 1. Pan-sear a 150g fillet of fish (like cod) in 1 tbsp of sesame oil until cooked. Set aside. 2. In the same pan, add 1/4 chopped onion and 1/2 chopped tomato. Sauté until soft. 3. Add 1/2 tsp ginger-garlic paste, 1/2 tsp cumin powder, and salt. Cook for 2 minutes. 4. Pour the masala over the seared fish.

Dinner

Meat (Mutton) Stew with Vegetables (7:30 PM)
Portion: 1 large bowl
Nutrition: 525 kcal, 9g Net Carbs, 45g Protein, 35g Fat, 6g Fiber
Recipe: 1. In a pressure cooker or pot, heat 1 tbsp sesame oil. Brown 150g of cubed meat (mutton/goat). 2. Add 1/2 chopped onion, 1 chopped tomato, 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste, 1/2 tsp turmeric, 1 tsp garam masala, and salt. Sauté for 5 minutes. 3. Add 1.5 cups of water. Pressure cook until meat is tender (approx. 20-25 mins). 4. Add 100g of chopped eggplant and 100g of chopped cabbage. Simmer for another 10 minutes until vegetables are cooked.

Snack

Two Hard-Boiled Eggs (4:00 PM)
Portion: 2 large eggs
Nutrition: 170 kcal, 3g Net Carbs, 6g Protein, 14g Fat, 0g Fiber
Recipe: 1. Boil two large eggs for 10 minutes. 2. Peel and season with salt and pepper.
