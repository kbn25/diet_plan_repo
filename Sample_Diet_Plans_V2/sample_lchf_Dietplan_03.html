7-Day Indian LCHF Vegan Meal Plan

This is a low-carbohydrate, high-fat vegan meal plan inspired by Indian cuisine, designed to align with specific dietary goals. Please review all ingredients to ensure they comply with your personal dietary restrictions.

Plan Specifications

Target Daily Calories ~1750 kcal
Target Net Carbs 25-30g
Allergens to Avoid Nuts, Dairy, Egg
Other Food Restrictions No Meat
Other Medical Conditions None specified

Day 1

Daily Totals

Calories1785 kcal
Net Carbs25g
Protein55g
Fat153g
Fiber43g

Breakfast: Coconut Chia Seed Pudding (8:30 AM)

Recipe: In a jar, mix 3 tbsp Chia seeds with 1 cup of full-fat coconut milk. Let it sit overnight or for at least 4 hours. Garnish with 5-6 fresh raspberries before serving.

Appetizer: Avocado and Cucumber Salad (12:45 PM)

Recipe: Dice 1/2 a medium Avocado (75g) and 1/2 a medium Cucumber (100g). Mix with 1 tbsp of lime juice, chopped fresh coriander, and a pinch of salt.

Lunch: Palak Mushroom Sabzi (Spinach & Mushroom) (1:00 PM)

Recipe: Heat 2 tbsp of coconut oil in a pan. Add 1/2 chopped Onion and sauté until translucent. Add 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste. Add 1.5 cups of sliced white Mushrooms (150g) and cook for 5 minutes. Add 2 cups of chopped Spinach (60g) and cook until wilted. Season with turmeric, cumin powder, and salt. Stir in 2 tbsp of hemp seeds for extra protein and texture.

Snack: Roasted Pumpkin Seeds (4:30 PM)

Recipe: 1/4 cup of raw pumpkin seeds (30g), optionally roasted with a pinch of salt.

Dinner: Cauliflower 'Rice' Pulao (7:30 PM)

Recipe: Grate 2 cups of Cauliflower florets (200g) to a rice-like consistency. Heat 2 tbsp coconut oil in a pan. Sauté 1/4 cup of chopped green beans for 2 minutes. Add the cauliflower 'rice' and cook for 5-7 minutes until tender. Season with salt and garam masala. Garnish with fresh coriander.

Day 2

Daily Totals

Calories1745 kcal
Net Carbs30g
Protein52g
Fat150g
Fiber35g

Breakfast: LCHF Flaxseed 'Upma' (8:30 AM)

Recipe: Heat 1 tbsp coconut oil. Sauté 1/4 finely chopped onion and 1 chopped green chili. Add 1.5 cups of water and bring to a boil. Slowly whisk in 1/2 cup of ground flaxseed. Cook for 2-3 minutes until it thickens. Season with salt and a squeeze of lime juice.

Appetizer: Simple Tomato Salad (12:45 PM)

Recipe: Slice 1 medium Tomato (120g). Arrange on a plate and drizzle with 1 tsp of sesame oil. Sprinkle with black salt and chopped fresh mint leaves (if available) or cilantro.

Lunch: Cabbage and Bell Pepper Sabzi (1:00 PM)

Recipe: Heat 2 tbsp coconut oil. Add cumin seeds. Sauté 1/2 sliced onion. Add 1 sliced red Bell Pepper (120g) and cook for 3 minutes. Add 2 cups of shredded Cabbage (150g), turmeric, and salt. Cook until tender. Garnish with 2 tbsp of shredded fresh coconut.

Snack: Fresh Coconut Chunks (4:30 PM)

Recipe: 1/2 cup (40g) of raw, fresh coconut chunks.

Dinner: Bhindi Fry (Spiced Okra) (7:30 PM)

Recipe: Wash and pat dry 1.5 cups of Okra (150g), then slice into rounds. Heat 2 tbsp of coconut oil. Sauté the okra on medium-high heat until crisp and cooked through (about 10-12 minutes). Sprinkle with chili powder, amchur (dry mango powder), and salt. Mix well and serve.

Snack: Avocado with Salt (9:00 PM)

Recipe: Slice half a medium avocado (75g) and sprinkle with rock salt.

Day 3

Daily Totals

Calories1710 kcal
Net Carbs25g
Protein60g
Fat145g
Fiber37g

Breakfast: Green Keto Smoothie (8:30 AM)

Recipe: Blend together: 1/2 avocado (75g), 1 cup kale, 3 tbsp hemp seeds, 1 tbsp ground flaxseed, 1 cup water, and 1/2 cup coconut milk. Blend until very smooth.

Appetizer: Chayote Sticks with Lime (12:45 PM)

Recipe: Peel and slice 1 medium Chayote fruit (150g) into sticks. Squeeze fresh lime juice over them and add a pinch of chili powder and salt.

Lunch: Green Bean Thoran (1:00 PM)

Recipe: Finely chop 1.5 cups of Green Beans (150g). Heat 2 tbsp coconut oil, splutter mustard seeds (if allowed) or cumin seeds, add chopped onion and green chili. Add the green beans, turmeric, and salt. Cover and cook until tender. Finally, add 1/3 cup of freshly grated Coconut and mix well.

Snack: Raw Sunflower Seeds (4:30 PM)

Recipe: 1/4 cup (30g) of raw sunflower seeds.

Dinner: Stuffed Bitter Gourd (Karela) (7:30 PM)

Recipe: Slit 2 medium Bitter Gourds (200g) and deseed. Rub with salt and set aside for 20 mins, then wash. For the stuffing, mix 1/2 finely chopped onion, 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste, 1/4 cup crushed sunflower seeds, chili powder, turmeric, and salt. Stuff the gourds. Pan-fry in 2 tbsp of coconut oil on low heat, covered, until tender.

Day 4

Daily Totals

Calories1690 kcal
Net Carbs30g
Protein58g
Fat140g
Fiber40g

Breakfast: Broccoli 'Saag' with Hemp Seeds (8:30 AM)

Recipe: Steam 2 cups of Broccoli florets (150g) until tender. Blend into a coarse paste. Heat 1 tbsp coconut oil, sauté 1/2 chopped onion, ginger, and garlic. Add the broccoli paste, 3 tbsp of hemp seeds, 1/4 cup coconut cream, and salt. Simmer for 5 minutes.

Appetizer: Fennel and Cucumber Salad (12:45 PM)

Recipe: Thinly slice 1/2 a Fennel bulb (100g) and 1/2 a Cucumber (100g). Toss with a dressing made of 1 tbsp sesame oil, 1 tbsp lime juice, and a pinch of salt.

Lunch: Asparagus and Sesame Stir-fry (1:00 PM)

Recipe: Chop 1 bunch of Asparagus (200g) into 2-inch pieces. Heat 2 tbsp sesame oil in a wok. Stir-fry the asparagus with sliced garlic and ginger for 4-5 minutes until tender-crisp. Season with salt and toss with 2 tbsp of toasted sesame seeds.

Snack: Avocado with Chia Seeds (4:30 PM)

Recipe: Mash half an avocado (75g) and mix in 1 tbsp of chia seeds. Add a pinch of salt and lime juice.

Dinner: Spiced Roasted Brussels Sprouts (7:30 PM)

Recipe: Toss 1.5 cups of Brussels Sprouts (150g), halved, with 2 tbsp of coconut oil, turmeric, chili powder, and salt. Roast at 200°C (400°F) for 20-25 minutes, until crispy and tender.

Day 5

Daily Totals

Calories1750 kcal
Net Carbs28g
Protein55g
Fat152g
Fiber38g

Breakfast: Avocado and Coconut Cream (8:30 AM)

Recipe: Blend 1 full medium avocado (150g) with 1/2 cup of coconut cream and a splash of water until smooth like a mousse. Sweeten with a few drops of stevia if desired (optional) or add a pinch of salt for a savory version.

Appetizer: Arugula Salad with Lemon Dressing (12:45 PM)

Recipe: Toss 1 cup of Arugula with 5-6 cherry tomatoes (halved). Drizzle with a dressing of 1 tbsp coconut oil, 1 tbsp lemon juice, and salt.

Lunch: Amaranth Greens with Coconut (1:00 PM)

Recipe: Heat 2 tbsp of coconut oil. Sauté 1/2 chopped onion and garlic. Add 3 cups of chopped Amaranth Leaves (100g) and cook until wilted. Season with salt and mix in 1/4 cup of grated fresh coconut.

Snack: Spiced Pumpkin Seeds (4:30 PM)

Recipe: Toss 1/4 cup of pumpkin seeds (30g) with a pinch of chili powder, salt, and 1/2 tsp of coconut oil. Roast for 10 minutes at 180°C (350°F).

Dinner: Lauki (Gourd) Coconut Curry (7:30 PM)

Recipe: Peel and cube 2 cups of White-flowered Gourd (Lauki, 250g). In a pot, heat 2 tbsp coconut oil. Sauté onion, ginger, and garlic. Add turmeric, cumin powder, and the gourd. Add 1 cup of coconut milk and 1/2 cup of water. Simmer until the gourd is tender. Season with salt.

Day 6

Daily Totals

Calories1725 kcal
Net Carbs25g
Protein65g
Fat148g
Fiber30g

Breakfast: Mushroom 'Keema' (8:30 AM)

Recipe: Finely chop or mince 2 cups of Portabella mushrooms (200g). Heat 2 tbsp coconut oil, sauté 1 finely chopped onion until brown. Add ginger-garlic paste and 1 chopped tomato. Cook until soft. Add the minced mushrooms, turmeric, chili powder, and salt. Cook until all moisture evaporates. Garnish with cilantro.

Appetizer: Radicchio and Cucumber Salad (12:45 PM)

Recipe: Shred 1 cup of Radicchio and slice 1/2 a Cucumber. Toss with a simple lime and salt dressing.

Lunch: Waxgourd Kootu (Stew) (1:00 PM)

Recipe: Boil 2 cups of cubed Waxgourd (250g) with turmeric and salt until tender. In a blender, make a paste of 1/3 cup coconut, 1 green chili, and a pinch of cumin seeds. Add this paste to the cooked gourd. Simmer for 5 minutes. Prepare a tempering of 1 tbsp coconut oil and mustard seeds (if allowed) and add to the kootu.

Snack: Flaxseed Crackers (4:30 PM)

Recipe: Mix 1/2 cup ground flaxseed with 1/4 cup water and a pinch of salt. Let it sit for 10 mins. Roll the dough very thin between two parchment papers. Score into cracker shapes. Bake at 180°C (350°F) for 15-20 mins until crisp. This recipe makes multiple servings; portion is for ~40g of the baked crackers.

Dinner: Grilled Avocado Halves (7:30 PM)

Recipe: Cut 1 medium Avocado (150g) in half and remove the pit. Brush with coconut oil and sprinkle with chili flakes and salt. Grill cut-side down for 3-4 minutes. Serve warm with a squeeze of lime.

Day 7

Daily Totals

Calories1790 kcal
Net Carbs28g
Protein56g
Fat155g
Fiber41g

Breakfast: Avocado on Mushroom 'Toast' (8:30 AM)

Recipe: Take 2 large Portabella mushroom caps, remove stems. Brush with oil and roast or grill for 10 minutes. Mash 1/2 an avocado (75g) with salt and lime juice. Spread the mashed avocado on the grilled mushroom caps. Sprinkle with chili flakes.

Appetizer: Wilted Swiss Chard Salad (12:45 PM)

Recipe: Roughly chop 2 cups of Swiss Chard. Heat 1 tbsp coconut oil and briefly sauté the chard with sliced garlic for 1-2 minutes until just wilted. Season with salt and lemon juice.

Lunch: Collard Greens 'Saag' (1:00 PM)

Recipe: Wash and finely chop 1 large bunch of Collard Greens (250g). Pressure cook or boil with a little ginger and garlic until very soft. Blend into a coarse puree. Heat 2 tbsp coconut oil, sauté chopped onion. Add the puree, 1/4 cup coconut cream, and salt. Simmer for 10 minutes.

Snack: Chia Fresca Drink (4:30 PM)

Recipe: Mix 2 tbsp of chia seeds in a large glass of cold water. Let it sit for 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally. Squeeze in the juice of half a lime.

Dinner: Artichoke Coconut Curry (7:30 PM)

Recipe: Use 1 can of artichoke hearts in water (drained), approx 250g. Heat 2 tbsp coconut oil. Sauté 1/2 onion, 1 chopped jalapeno, and ginger-garlic paste. Add turmeric and cumin powder. Add the artichoke hearts and 1 cup of coconut milk. Simmer for 10-15 minutes. Season with salt.
