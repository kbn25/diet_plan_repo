7-Day LCHF Indian-Style Meal Plan

Plan Details

Target Audience: 40-year-old person with Type 2 Diabetes (BMI: 20.0)

Diet Type: LCHF (Low-Carb, High-Fat), Pescetarian

Cuisine: Indian-Style

Daily Target: ~1600-1800 kcal, < 40g Net Carbs

Food List & Restrictions

Allowed Foods: Artichoke, Arugula, Broccoli, Cabbage, Cauliflower, Chard, Eggplant, Eggs, Fish, Garlic, Ginger, Kale, Lettuce, Mushrooms, Onion, Sesame seeds, Spinach, Strawberry, Tomatoes

Allergies: Gluten, Nuts, Dairy

Other Restrictions: No Meat/Poultry

Note: This meal plan is designed for blood sugar management and weight maintenance. Flavor is built using garlic, ginger, onions, and tomatoes. It is assumed that sesame oil is used for cooking and basic seasonings like salt and pepper are permitted. All nutritional values are estimates. Drink plenty of water throughout the day.

Day 1
Daily Totals: 1685 kcal | Carbs: 48g | Protein: 92g | Fat: 129g | Fiber: 15g

Pre-Meal Appetizer

Simple Arugula Salad (12:50 PM)
Nutrition: 75 kcal, Carbs: 2g, Protein: 1g, Fat: 7g, Fiber: 1g
Recipe: Toss 1 cup of arugula with 2 tsp of sesame oil and a pinch of salt.

Main Meals

Breakfast: Masala Omelette (8:00 AM)
Main Ingredient: Eggs
Nutrition: 321 kcal, Carbs: 6g, Protein: 15g, Fat: 27g, Fiber: 2g
Recipe: Whisk 2 large eggs. Sauté 1 tbsp chopped onion, 1/2 tsp each of minced garlic and ginger in 1 tbsp sesame oil. Add 1/4 chopped tomato and 1/2 cup chopped spinach and cook until wilted. Pour in the eggs, season with salt, and cook until set.
Lunch: Pan-Seared Fish with Steamed Broccoli (1:00 PM)
Main Ingredient: Fish
Nutrition: 529 kcal, Carbs: 8g, Protein: 46g, Fat: 35g, Fiber: 4g
Recipe: Season a 170g (6 oz) fatty fish fillet (like salmon) with salt, minced garlic, and ginger. Pan-sear in 1 tbsp sesame oil for 4-5 minutes per side. Serve with 1.5 cups of steamed broccoli florets.
Dinner: Baingan Bharta (Smoky Eggplant Mash) (7:00 PM)
Main Ingredient: Eggplant
Nutrition: 660 kcal, Carbs: 27g, Protein: 28g, Fat: 55g, Fiber: 7g
Recipe: Roast 1 medium eggplant (400g) over a flame or in the oven until skin is charred and flesh is soft. Cool, peel, and mash. In a pan, heat 2 tbsp sesame oil, sauté 1/2 chopped onion, 1 tsp each of minced garlic and ginger. Add 1 chopped tomato and cook until soft. Add the mashed eggplant, salt, and cook for 5-7 minutes. Serve with 2 scrambled eggs (cooked in 1 tbsp of sesame oil) on the side for protein.

Snack

Strawberries with Sesame Seeds (4:00 PM)
Nutrition: 100 kcal, Carbs: 9g, Protein: 2g, Fat: 7g, Fiber: 1g
Recipe: Slice 1/2 cup of strawberries and top with 1 tbsp of toasted sesame seeds.
Day 2
Daily Totals: 1653 kcal | Carbs: 49g | Protein: 85g | Fat: 127g | Fiber: 19g

Pre-Meal Appetizer

Tomato-Garlic Bites (6:50 PM)
Nutrition: 82 kcal, Carbs: 6g, Protein: 1g, Fat: 6g, Fiber: 1g
Recipe: Halve 1/2 cup of cherry tomatoes. Toss with 1 minced garlic clove, 1.5 tsp sesame oil, and salt.

Main Meals

Breakfast: Cauliflower Upma-Style Stir-fry (8:00 AM)
Main Ingredient: Cauliflower
Nutrition: 425 kcal, Carbs: 18g, Protein: 28g, Fat: 29g, Fiber: 8g
Recipe: Grate 2 cups of cauliflower. Heat 1 tbsp sesame oil, sauté 1/4 chopped onion, 1 tsp each of minced garlic and ginger. Add the cauliflower 'rice', salt, and stir-fry for 5-7 minutes until tender. Serve topped with 2 fried eggs (cooked in 1 tbsp sesame oil).
Lunch: Mushroom and Spinach Sabzi (1:00 PM)
Main Ingredient: Mushrooms
Nutrition: 536 kcal, Carbs: 15g, Protein: 45g, Fat: 35g, Fiber: 6g
Recipe: Heat 1 tbsp sesame oil. Sauté 1/4 chopped onion and 1 tsp minced garlic. Add 2 cups of sliced mushrooms and cook until browned. Add 2 cups of spinach and 1/2 chopped tomato, cooking until spinach wilts. Season with salt. Serve with a 170g (6oz) pan-seared fish fillet (cooked in 1 tbsp sesame oil) on the side.
Dinner: Garlic Sautéed Chard (7:00 PM)
Main Ingredient: Chard
Nutrition: 510 kcal, Carbs: 8g, Protein: 10g, Fat: 47g, Fiber: 3g
Recipe: Wash and chop 4 cups of chard. In a large pan, heat 2 tbsp of sesame oil and sauté 3 cloves of minced garlic until fragrant. Add the chard and cook until wilted and tender (about 5-7 minutes). Season with salt. Serve topped with 2 tbsp of sesame seeds and 2 hard-boiled eggs on the side.

Snack

Simple Lettuce Wraps (4:00 PM)
Nutrition: 100 kcal, Carbs: 2g, Protein: 1g, Fat: 10g, Fiber: 1g
Recipe: Take 2 large lettuce leaves. Finely chop 1/4 cup of mushrooms and mix with 2 tsp sesame oil and a pinch of salt. Use as a filling for the lettuce wraps.
Day 3
Daily Totals: 1711 kcal | Carbs: 46g | Protein: 109g | Fat: 127g | Fiber: 17g

Pre-Meal Appetizer

Spinach & Garlic Bites (12:50 PM)
Nutrition: 85 kcal, Carbs: 2g, Protein: 2g, Fat: 8g, Fiber: 1g
Recipe: Briefly sauté 1 cup of spinach with 1 minced garlic clove in 2 tsp sesame oil until just wilted.

Main Meals

Breakfast: Scrambled Eggs with Cabbage (8:00 AM)
Main Ingredient: Cabbage
Nutrition: 381 kcal, Carbs: 12g, Protein: 18g, Fat: 30g, Fiber: 5g
Recipe: Heat 1 tbsp sesame oil in a pan. Sauté 1 cup of shredded cabbage with 1/4 chopped onion until tender. Whisk 2 large eggs with salt and pour over the cabbage. Scramble until cooked.
Lunch: Fish with Roasted Broccoli (1:00 PM)
Main Ingredient: Fish
Nutrition: 595 kcal, Carbs: 11g, Protein: 48g, Fat: 41g, Fiber: 5g
Recipe: Toss 2 cups of broccoli florets with 1 tbsp sesame oil and minced garlic. Roast at 200°C (400°F) for 15-20 minutes. Serve with a 170g (6 oz) fatty fish fillet (like salmon), pan-seared in 1 tbsp sesame oil.
Dinner: Tomato 'Rasam' Style Soup (7:00 PM)
Main Ingredient: Tomatoes
Nutrition: 550 kcal, Carbs: 15g, Protein: 39g, Fat: 38g, Fiber: 5g
Recipe: Sauté 1/2 chopped onion, 1 tsp each of minced garlic and ginger in 1 tbsp sesame oil. Add 2 large chopped tomatoes (300g) and 2 cups of water. Simmer for 15 minutes. Blend until smooth. Season with salt. Serve with a 170g (6oz) baked fish fillet on the side, seasoned with salt and garlic.

Snack

Sliced Strawberries (4:00 PM)
Nutrition: 100 kcal, Carbs: 6g, Protein: 2g, Fat: 10g, Fiber: 1g
Recipe: Enjoy 1 cup of fresh sliced strawberries with 2 tsp of sesame oil drizzled on top for added fats.
Day 4
Daily Totals: 1699 kcal | Carbs: 45g | Protein: 90g | Fat: 133g | Fiber: 18g

Pre-Meal Appetizer

Mushroom Skewers (6:50 PM)
Nutrition: 80 kcal, Carbs: 3g, Protein: 2g, Fat: 7g, Fiber: 1g
Recipe: Marinate 4-5 button mushrooms in 2 tsp sesame oil, minced garlic, and salt. Thread onto skewers and bake or pan-fry until tender.

Main Meals

Breakfast: Cheesy Egg Nests (8:00 AM)
Main Ingredient: Eggs
Nutrition: 399 kcal, Carbs: 13g, Protein: 21g, Fat: 30g, Fiber: 6g
Recipe: Grate 1 cup of cauliflower and mix with 1 egg and 1/4 cup of shredded low-carb cheese (if available, otherwise use 1 tbsp sesame seeds for texture). Form two 'nests' on a baking sheet. Bake for 10 mins at 200°C (400°F). Crack an egg into the center of each nest and bake for another 5-7 mins. Serve with 1/2 cup of arugula.
Lunch: Kale and Mushroom Stir-fry with Fish (1:00 PM)
Main Ingredient: Kale
Nutrition: 610 kcal, Carbs: 14g, Protein: 47g, Fat: 43g, Fiber: 5g
Recipe: Heat 1 tbsp sesame oil. Sauté 1 tsp minced garlic. Add 1 cup sliced mushrooms and cook. Add 3 cups of chopped kale and stir-fry until wilted. Season with salt. Serve with a 170g (6 oz) pan-seared fish fillet (cooked in 1 tbsp sesame oil).
Dinner: Stuffed Artichoke Hearts (7:00 PM)
Main Ingredient: Artichoke
Nutrition: 510 kcal, Carbs: 13g, Protein: 18g, Fat: 43g, Fiber: 5g
Recipe: Use 4 canned artichoke hearts in water, drained and halved. Create a stuffing by mixing 2 hard-boiled chopped eggs, 1 tbsp sesame seeds, minced garlic, and 2 tbsp sesame oil. Stuff the artichoke hearts with the mixture. Bake at 180°C (350°F) for 10 minutes.

Snack

Tomato Slices with Sesame Seeds (4:00 PM)
Nutrition: 100 kcal, Carbs: 2g, Protein: 2g, Fat: 10g, Fiber: 1g
Recipe: Slice one medium tomato and sprinkle with salt and 1 tbsp of sesame seeds.
Day 5
Daily Totals: 1678 kcal | Carbs: 47g | Protein: 104g | Fat: 124g | Fiber: 18g

Pre-Meal Appetizer

Arugula and Tomato Salad (12:50 PM)
Nutrition: 80 kcal, Carbs: 4g, Protein: 1g, Fat: 7g, Fiber: 1g
Recipe: Toss 1 cup of arugula and 1/4 cup of cherry tomatoes with 2 tsp sesame oil and salt.

Main Meals

Breakfast: Cauliflower Hash with Fried Egg (8:00 AM)
Main Ingredient: Cauliflower
Nutrition: 398 kcal, Carbs: 13g, Protein: 15g, Fat: 32g, Fiber: 6g
Recipe: Dice 1.5 cups of cauliflower florets. Sauté 1/4 chopped onion in 1 tbsp sesame oil until soft. Add cauliflower and cook until tender and slightly browned. Season with salt, garlic, and ginger. Top with one large egg fried in 1 tbsp sesame oil.
Lunch: Spinach with Pan-Seared Fish (1:00 PM)
Main Ingredient: Spinach
Nutrition: 605 kcal, Carbs: 8g, Protein: 49g, Fat: 43g, Fiber: 5g
Recipe: In a pan, heat 1 tbsp sesame oil and sauté 2 cloves of minced garlic. Add 4 cups of fresh spinach and cook until wilted. Season with salt. Serve with a 170g (6 oz) fatty fish fillet, pan-seared in 1 tbsp sesame oil.
Dinner: Egg Curry (No-Spice Version) (7:00 PM)
Main Ingredient: Eggs
Nutrition: 500 kcal, Carbs: 13g, Protein: 37g, Fat: 32g, Fiber: 5g
Recipe: Hard-boil 2 eggs and peel. Make a sauce by sautéing 1/2 chopped onion, 1 tsp each of minced garlic and ginger in 1 tbsp sesame oil. Add 1.5 chopped tomatoes and cook into a paste. Add 1/2 cup water and simmer. Add the hard-boiled eggs (halved). Serve with 1/2 an avocado on the side for fat and creaminess.

Snack

Strawberries (4:00 PM)
Nutrition: 95 kcal, Carbs: 9g, Protein: 2g, Fat: 10g, Fiber: 1g
Recipe: Enjoy 1 cup of fresh sliced strawberries.
Day 6
Daily Totals: 1645 kcal | Carbs: 50g | Protein: 86g | Fat: 127g | Fiber: 21g

Pre-Meal Appetizer

Lettuce Cups with Chopped Tomato (12:50 PM)
Nutrition: 75 kcal, Carbs: 4g, Protein: 1g, Fat: 6g, Fiber: 1g
Recipe: Use 2 large lettuce leaves as cups. Fill with 1/4 cup chopped tomato mixed with 1.5 tsp sesame oil and salt.

Main Meals

Breakfast: Broccoli and Egg Stir-fry (8:00 AM)
Main Ingredient: Broccoli
Nutrition: 365 kcal, Carbs: 13g, Protein: 18g, Fat: 28g, Fiber: 6g
Recipe: Steam 1.5 cups of broccoli florets until tender-crisp. In a wok, heat 1 tbsp sesame oil. Add the broccoli and stir-fry with minced garlic and ginger. Push to one side, add 1 tbsp sesame oil and scramble 2 large eggs. Mix everything together.
Lunch: Eggplant and Mushroom Sabzi with Fish (1:00 PM)
Main Ingredient: Eggplant
Nutrition: 615 kcal, Carbs: 20g, Protein: 45g, Fat: 43g, Fiber: 9g
Recipe: Chop 1 small eggplant (200g) and 1 cup of mushrooms. Heat 1 tbsp sesame oil, sauté 1/4 onion, garlic, and ginger. Add eggplant and mushrooms, and cook until tender. Add 1/2 chopped tomato and salt. Serve with a 170g (6 oz) pan-seared fish fillet (cooked in 1 tbsp sesame oil).
Dinner: Hearty Fish and Tomato Stew (7:00 PM)
Main Ingredient: Fish
Nutrition: 500 kcal, Carbs: 11g, Protein: 20g, Fat: 40g, Fiber: 4g
Recipe: In a pot, sauté 1/4 chopped onion and 1 tsp garlic in 1 tbsp sesame oil. Add 1.5 chopped tomatoes and 1.5 cups of water. Simmer for 10 minutes. Add 120g (4 oz) of cubed fatty fish and simmer for another 5-7 minutes until cooked through. Season with salt. Stir in 1 tbsp of sesame oil before serving for extra fat.

Snack

Sautéed Mushrooms (4:00 PM)
Nutrition: 90 kcal, Carbs: 2g, Protein: 2g, Fat: 10g, Fiber: 1g
Recipe: Sauté 1 cup of sliced mushrooms in 2 tsp sesame oil with a pinch of garlic powder and salt until golden.
Day 7
Daily Totals: 1698 kcal | Carbs: 48g | Protein: 96g | Fat: 130g | Fiber: 19g

Pre-Meal Appetizer

Ginger-Garlic Cabbage Salad (6:50 PM)
Nutrition: 88 kcal, Carbs: 5g, Protein: 1g, Fat: 7g, Fiber: 2g
Recipe: Finely shred 1 cup of cabbage. Make a dressing with 2 tsp sesame oil, 1/2 tsp each of minced ginger and garlic, and salt. Toss with the cabbage.

Main Meals

Breakfast: Spinach and Tomato Scrambled Eggs (8:00 AM)
Main Ingredient: Eggs
Nutrition: 396 kcal, Carbs: 7g, Protein: 16g, Fat: 34g, Fiber: 2g
Recipe: Heat 1 tbsp sesame oil. Sauté 1/4 chopped tomato and 1 cup of spinach until wilted. Pour in 2 whisked eggs and scramble. Top with 1 tbsp of sesame seeds.
Lunch: Baked Fish with Sautéed Chard (1:00 PM)
Main Ingredient: Fish
Nutrition: 614 kcal, Carbs: 11g, Protein: 49g, Fat: 43g, Fiber: 5g
Recipe: Bake a 170g (6 oz) fatty fish fillet seasoned with garlic and salt at 200°C (400°F) for 12-15 minutes. While it bakes, sauté 3 cups of chopped chard with 1 tsp minced garlic in 1 tbsp of sesame oil. Serve fish over the bed of chard.
Dinner: Cabbage and Egg Drop Soup (7:00 PM)
Main Ingredient: Cabbage
Nutrition: 500 kcal, Carbs: 16g, Protein: 28g, Fat: 36g, Fiber: 9g
Recipe: Bring 3 cups of water to a boil with 1 tsp each of minced ginger and garlic. Add 2 cups of shredded cabbage and cook for 5 minutes. Slowly drizzle in 2 whisked eggs while stirring to create ribbons. Turn off heat, stir in 2 tbsp sesame oil and salt.

Snack

Strawberry and Sesame Treat (4:00 PM)
Nutrition: 100 kcal, Carbs: 9g, Protein: 2g, Fat: 10g, Fiber: 1g
Recipe: Mash 1/2 cup of strawberries and mix with 1 tbsp of sesame seeds.
