7-Day Oil-Free LCHF Meal Plan

Plan Overview

Target Profile

A comprehensive, medically-safe, oil-free LCHF meal plan for a 40-year-old individual with Type 2 Diabetes and a BMI of 20.0, designed for weight maintenance. This plan is strictly dairy-free, soy-free, and chicken-free.

LCHF Approach

Therapeutic ketogenic diet designed to promote nutritional ketosis for enhanced blood glucose stability and improved insulin sensitivity. All fats are derived from whole food sources, strictly excluding all extracted oils. The plan is structured around authentic Indian flavors using LCHF-compliant ingredients and traditional, oil-free cooking methods.

CRITICAL MEDICAL DISCLAIMER

This meal plan is for informational purposes only and is not a substitute for professional medical advice. The individual must consult with their physician or a registered dietitian before starting this or any new diet plan, especially with a pre-existing condition like Type 2 Diabetes. Medications may need to be adjusted under medical supervision to prevent hypoglycemia. Blood glucose levels should be monitored closely.
