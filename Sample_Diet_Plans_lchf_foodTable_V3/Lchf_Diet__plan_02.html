Day 1

Total Calories: 1821 Net Carbs: 25.1g Ketogenic Ratio: 2.2:1

Breakfast (8:00 AM) - Avocado & Coconut 'Poha'

Preparation:

Finely grate 150g cauliflower. Dry roast 1 tsp of mustard seeds in a pan until they pop. Add finely chopped green chili and 10g spring onions and water-sauté for a minute. Add the grated cauliflower, turmeric, and salt. Sauté with splashes of water until tender-crisp. Turn off the heat. Mash 100g of avocado and mix it into the cauliflower along with 50g of fresh, grated raw coconut meat and a squeeze of lemon juice. Garnish with fresh coriander.

Ingredients:

  • Cauliflower, raw (150g)
  • Avocados, raw, all commercial varieties (100g)
  • Nuts, coconut meat, raw (50g)
  • Onions, spring or scallions (10g)
  • Coriander (cilantro) leaves, raw (5g)
  • Lemon juice, raw (5ml)
  • Peppers, hot chili, green, raw (5g)

Nutrition:

  • Calories: 441
  • Net Carbs: 8.6g
  • Protein: 7.9g
  • Fat: 39.8g

Lunch (1:00 PM) - Oil-Free Goat Bhuna with Spinach

Preparation:

In a pressure cooker, dry sear 150g of goat meat until browned to render its natural fat. Remove the meat. In the same pot, water-sauté 50g chopped onions, 10g ginger-garlic paste until fragrant. Add 50g chopped tomatoes and cook until soft. Add turmeric, red chili powder, and ground coriander/cumin. Return the goat meat to the pot, add salt and 1/2 cup of water. Pressure cook until tender (approx. 20-25 mins). Open the cooker and sauté on high heat to reduce the gravy until it clings to the meat. Serve with 150g of steamed spinach on the side.

Ingredients:

  • Game meat, goat, raw (150g)
  • Onions, yellow, raw (50g)
  • Tomatoes, red, ripe, raw (50g)
  • Ginger root, raw (5g)
  • Garlic, raw (5g)
  • Spinach, mature (150g)

Nutrition:

  • Calories: 258
  • Net Carbs: 6.1g
  • Protein: 36.3g
  • Fat: 6.8g

Dinner (7:30 PM) - Tandoori-Style Chicken Skewers with Mint Raita

Preparation:

Marinate 150g chicken pieces in a paste of 20g ground sesame seeds, lemon juice, ginger-garlic paste, garam masala, and salt for at least 30 minutes. Thread onto skewers and grill or bake at 200°C (400°F) until cooked through. For the 'raita', finely chop 100g cucumber and mix with 30g of ground sesame seed paste, fresh mint leaves, salt, and water to create a creamy, dairy-free sauce.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken, stewing, meat only, raw (150g)
  • Seeds, sesame seeds, whole, dried (50g)
  • Cucumber, with peel, raw (100g)
  • Lemon juice, raw (10ml)
  • Ginger root, raw (5g)
  • Garlic, raw (5g)
