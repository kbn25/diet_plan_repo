Due to the oil-free constraint and the leanness of the approved meats, the fat percentage is maintained at the lower end of the target therapeutic range (~68-70%) to ensure nutritional adequacy from the provided ingredient list. All allergens (nuts, fish) and restrictions (coconut) have been strictly excluded.

Dinner (7:30 PM): Dry-Fried Chicken with Brussels Sprouts

Preparation: Halve the Brussels sprouts. In a hot, dry pan, place sprouts cut-side down and allow them to char slightly. Add a splash of water and cover to steam until tender. Set aside. In the same pan, cook cubed chicken until done. Return sprouts to the pan. Serve topped with sliced avocado.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken, stewing, meat only, raw: 150 g
  • Avocados, raw, all commercial varieties: 150 g
  • Brussels sprouts, raw: 150 g
Nutrition: 486 kcal | 41.9g Fat | 30.3g Protein | 4.1g Net Carbs | 22.1g Fiber | Ketogenic Ratio: 0.7:1 | Blood Glucose Impact: Very Low
